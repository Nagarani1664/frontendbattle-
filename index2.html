üìå Mandatory Features for a Valid Submission

For your submission to be considered valid and move forward to the judging phase, it must meet the following minimum criteria:

  1. RESPONSIVENESS: Any layout breakout or non-responsiveness will be severely punished.
  2. FEATURES: At least 6 features among the sample space must be implemented.

üìã Judging Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated based on the following:

  • Design Accuracy (40%): How closely the submission matches the provided design mockups and assets.
  • Responsiveness (25%): How well the layout adapts to different screen sizes and orientations.
  • Innovation/Enhancements (20%): The quality and impact of any creative additions or improvements beyond the basic replication.
    (e.g., subtle animations, improved accessibility features, minor functional enhancements)
  • Code Quality & Best Practices (15%): Readability, organization, adherence to web standards, and efficient use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
